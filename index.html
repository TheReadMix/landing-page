The Science & The Culture

Why It Works

Science of Reading
Grounded in How the Brain Learns to Read
Aligned with the Science of Reading, we use the Orton-Gillingham approach to make instruction accessible to students with specific learning differences. This approach supports the one in five students with dyslexia who struggle the most.
Youth Culture Pedagogy
Teens Who Feel Seen Actually Learn
Youth culture isn’t decoration, it’s the delivery system. When students see their language, identities, and interests reflected in the material, they’re not just engaged, they’re invested. That ownership transforms how they show up to learning.
Our Story

A record-breaker who almost never learned to read.

Before The ReadMix was a curriculum, it was a way for a functionally illiterate high school kid to play with words and escape the boredom of not being able to access grade level text. This personal breakthrough became the blueprint for a literacy program built for students who learn differently, love creativity, and deserve lessons that meet them where they are.

Romane Armand
Founder & Reading Specialist
