The Stakes

Struggling readers don't need sympathy. They need a system that works.

2 in 3
Middle and high schoolers nationwide read below grade level — and most intervention programs aren't built for who they are.
4x
Students who aren't reading proficiently by third grade are up to four times more likely to leave school without a diploma.
1 in 5

The Science & The Culture

Why It Works

Science of Reading
Grounded in how the brain learns to read.
Aligned with the Science of Reading, the new standard for literacy instruction across many states. We use the Orton Gillingham approach, which makes this instruction accessible to students with specific learning differences like dyslexia.
Youth Culture Pedagogy
Teens Who Feel Seen Actually Learn
We reflect teen identities, language, and interests through co-designing with students. When students see themselves in the material, they take ownership in their learning. Engagement follows.
